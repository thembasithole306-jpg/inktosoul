Fragments of a Heart

Hey!

I'm writing this, not because I want to,
But because my chest has become too heavy to carry.
Every breath I take
Feels like another piece of me breaking,
Like a blade turning slowly
Inside a wound that never truly closed.

You promised me forever,
And I believed every word you spoke.
Not because you were perfect,
But because your voice felt like peace,
And your eyes held a kind of honesty
I thought would never fade.

Maybe I should have known
That people break promises they once meant to keep.
That love can disappear
Even while two hearts still beat.
And that goodbyes begin in silence,
Long before they are ever spoken.

I tried, I truly did.
I held on to every fragile piece of us,
Even when they cut through my hands.
I fought for something
That was already slipping away.
The moment you let go,
Everything else followed.

Now I'm here, writing these words,
Not to make you feel guilty,
Not to remind you of what we lost,
But to set myself free
From the burden of pretending
That I walked away untouched.

The truth is,
Some people leave with a goodbye.

You left with pieces of me
I still haven't found.

So if I seem different now,
It's because I'm still wandering
Through the ruins of what we were,
Searching for the fragments of a heart
That once beat only for you.
