Poem 2

Echoes of the Soul

Hey!

Yeah, I know, I know.

It's me again.

Don't worry, I'm not writing in pain,
Ever since I met you, I forgot what that feels like.
There's this constant motion inside my chest,
Like spinning wheels that never rest.

What I mean is that it never stops,
It keeps turning, climbing, rising, it hops.
It makes time wish it could stand still,
As if the world itself obeyed my will.

No feeling has ever been greater than this,
My heart keeps rising like an endless elevator.
My thoughts spin wildly like a restless operator,
Mind and soul agree, "Proceed with caution, danger."
Still love knocked once, a fearless instigator,
Leaving every answer clear as a calculator.

I still don't understand how your presence is so strong,
My eyes, my mind, my heart can't measure for long.
Are you a rainbow because you're vivid and bright?
Or a sunrise chasing away every night?
All I could do was admire the view,
While every heartbeat quietly whispered you.

I don't even want to start talking about your face,
The kind that needs no filter, only grace.
An Ace among beauty, gentle and serene,
The kind poets chase but have never seen.

Okay, let me cut the chase,
Be like a full stop and get straight to the place.
My heart is running a marathon race,
Every thought of you another checkpoint to embrace.

Maybe that's why these wheels never slow.
Maybe some hearts already know.
Before lips confess,
Before hands even touch,
Before love has a name.

Because ever since I met you,
Something changed inside my soul.

My heart still beats for me,

But every beat

Sounds a little more

Like you.
