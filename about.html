ABOUT

Behind every poem is a feeling. Behind every feeling is a story.

Behind the Words

INK TO SOUL (A place where words speak and hearts listen) is more than a poetry collection.

It is a place where emotions find language, where silence becomes expression, and where thoughts too heavy to be spoken are transformed into words that can be understood.

Every poem within this collection explores a different part of the human experience: Love, loss, hope, identity, healing, loneliness, and growth.

Rather than offering answers, these poems invite reflection. They encourage readers to pause, to feel, and to discover their own meaning within every verse.

The Writer

Themba Gift Sithole is a poet, writer, and the founder of INK TO SOUL.

His work is inspired by the belief that some emotions cannot be fully explained through conversation, but can be understood through poetry. His writing explores the depth and complexity of the human experience through poetry.

Instead of telling one person's story, his writing seeks to give a voice to feelings shared by many. Each poem is written with the intention of capturing emotions that often remain unspoken, allowing readers to find comfort, understanding, or even a reflection of themselves within the words.

He does not write to define emotion.

He writes to express it.

The Philosophy

Some feelings are universal.

A broken heart.

A silent victory.

The fear of being forgotten.

The hope of becoming enough.

INK TO SOUL exists because poetry has the power to reach places that ordinary conversations cannot.

Every poem is an invitation to feel, to reflect, and to remember that no emotion is ever truly carried alone.

Why Every Poem Begins With "Hey!"

That's a question I get a lot.

Every conversation begins with a simple greeting.

Every connection begins with a single moment.

That is why every poem in INK TO SOUL begins with "Hey!"

It is an invitation—not merely to read, but to listen.

Not merely to observe, but to feel.

It is one soul reaching out to another before the first verse is ever spoken.

A Final Word

Whether you arrived searching for comfort, understanding, or simply beautiful words, thank you for being part of this journey.

If even one poem helps you feel seen, understood, or less alone, then INK TO SOUL has fulfilled its purpose.

Because some feelings cannot be spoken.

So they became poetry.
