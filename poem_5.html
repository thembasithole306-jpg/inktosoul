Who Am I When I Am Not Me?

Hey!

I feel like I am made of anger, rage, and flame,
a silent kind of broken that nobody can name.
I smile in crowded places, but I am not okay,
I live inside a night where light has lost its way.

I feel like I am drowning, but I still have to stand,
like I am holding shattered pieces in my hand.
I want to leave this place, I want to disappear,
but even in my silence I am trapped right here.

I try to pray for peace, but words refuse to rise,
they fall back into silence behind tired eyes.
I open up the scripture, but it does not feel the same,
it feels like I am reading but forgetting every name.

I laugh so they won’t notice what is killing me inside,
a war behind my breathing I constantly must hide.
And still I keep on walking like nothing is undone,
while everything I am is slowly coming undone.

It feels like my heart was ripped and never given back,
like I am walking forward but I am off the track.
Is this my mind collapsing, or truth I cannot see,
or am I just a stranger trapped inside of me?

Who am I when silence is louder than my voice,
when every path I take feels like a stolen choice?
Am I my thoughts, my pain, or what they say I am,
or just a broken echo trying to understand?

Because right and wrong don’t look the way they should,
even poison feels like something misunderstood.
And still I think of others even when I break,
still caring for the world while I begin to shake.

What do I want, what do I feel, what is even real,
when every part of me forgets the way to heal?
I ask myself for answers but nothing answers back,
just footsteps of confusion walking down a cracked track.

The “me” I know is distant, like a shadow on the wall,
a body moving forward while I quietly fall.
And if I lose all feeling, will I become a beast,
or finally be released from everything I ceased?

Every choice feels heavy, every thought feels torn,
like I was built from battles I was never born.
Left feels like destruction, right feels like regret,
and standing still feels like a debt I cannot forget.

I am a smile made of silence, I am noise without a sound,
a scream that never leaves me even when no one’s around.
Wake me if I am dreaming, tell me this is not my fate,
because living in this mirror feels like endless weight.

All I hear are voices that tell me I will fail,
even when I stand still, even when I prevail.
Every step feels judged before I even move,
like I am always wrong no matter what I prove.

I am made of contradiction, I am sadness in disguise,
a storm that learned to hide itself behind its eyes.
No joy to balance pain, no peace to make things right,
just endless conversations with my own inner night.

And I wonder if I will rewrite what I became,
or stay inside a story that always ends the same.
Am I breaking into freedom or falling into shame,
or is this just the way I learn to know my name?
